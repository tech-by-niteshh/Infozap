The Vision Behind Infozap

A Convergence of IoT, Web Development, and Digital Journalism

A Milestone in Academic Excellence

Launched in 2026, Infozap stands as a flagship Web Development project within the B.Voc (Internet of Things) department. It was conceived not just as a website, but as a robust digital ecosystem designed to handle the rapid-fire nature of modern current affairs.

This project is a proud initiative of the students of Year 2026 B.Voc in Internet of Things (IoT) at Dayalbagh Educational Institute (DEI). It reflects the institute’s strong emphasis on practical learning, skill development and real-world application of technology. By integrating the core principles of IoT with modern web technologies, the project demonstrates the ability to build solutions that are scalable, efficient, and user-friendly. It highlights a hands-on approach to learning, where theoretical knowledge is effectively transformed into impactful, real-world systems.
